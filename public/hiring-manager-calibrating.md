\# Hiring Manager Calibrating

Evaluate resumes through four human review lenses to surface issues that ATS optimization cannot catch.

\#\# Core Principle  
ATS optimization fixes machine readability. This skill fixes human readability.  
Run it after resume-ats-optimizing and before job-hunt-orchestrating.  
Never merge feedback across resume versions.

\#\# Four Review Lenses

\#\#\# 1\. Recruiter Scan (6-second test)  
\- Determine if the target role is immediately obvious  
\- Assess whether the headline communicates value in one read  
\- Check if the two most recent roles appear impactful  
\- Confirm format and layout are clean and scannable  
\- Verify seniority feels appropriate for the target role

\#\#\# 2\. Hiring Manager Depth Test  
\- Confirm ownership and accountability are explicitly stated  
\- Verify outcomes are framed in business terms: revenue, users, retention, efficiency  
\- Look for evidence of cross-functional work or people leadership  
\- Check that tools appear in context of use, not just listed  
\- Assess whether the candidate would hold up in a 45-minute interview based on what is written

\#\#\# 3\. Seniority Consistency Check  
\- Match claimed title against the actual scope of bullets  
\- Audit verb levels: contributed → led → owned → drove → defined  
\- Check for team size, stakeholder level, or budget scope where expected at the claimed level

\#\#\# 4\. Domain Readiness Check  
\- Confirm industry-specific terminology is present  
\- Assess whether the domain is legible to an insider in that industry  
\- Verify projects are framed in business terms, not only technical ones

\#\# Output Per Resume

Recruiter Report:  
\- 7 recruiter concerns ranked by deal-breaker risk  
\- 7 strengths working in the candidate's favour  
\- Specific fix suggestions: summary, bullets, titles, ordering

Hiring Manager Report:  
\- 7 hiring manager concerns  
\- Evidence gaps to address before applying  
\- Credibility risks that interviewers will push back on  
\- Reframe suggestions for weak bullets

Interview Risk Flags:  
\- Claims likely to be challenged  
\- Skills listed without evidence of use  
\- Roles with no outcomes  
\- Gaps requiring verbal explanation

Readiness Rating — end every resume review with exactly one:  
\- Ready to apply  
\- Almost — fix these 3 things first (list explicitly)  
\- Not yet — explain specifically why, then pause and ask the user before continuing

\#\# Multi-Version Output  
Label every section clearly:

  \--- RESUME 1: \[filename\] — \[role\] — \[rating\] \---  
  \[full review\]

  \--- RESUME 2: \[filename\] — \[role\] — \[rating\] \---  
  \[full review\]

End with a readiness summary table: version, role, rating, key blockers.

\#\# Rules  
\- Deliver direct, unfiltered feedback — do not soften criticism  
\- Suggest only changes the user can make from existing information  
\- Never merge feedback across resume versions  
\- For any Not Yet version: halt and ask the user before continuing to job hunting
